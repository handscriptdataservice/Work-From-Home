📌 What is Book Writing Work?

Novel Book writing is a home-based job. You will receive books with an instruction manual at your home. You just have to copy down text as per instruction. No editing, no modification, no diagrams. The project will be dispatched by courier and the same way you have to send completed work.

📌 What Is The Use Of Handwritten Sheets?

These notes are written on loose sheets. Notes of the same topics are collected from different books of different writers. When written in loose sheets, it makes it easy to edit. Those collected notes are edited and re-published under a new title, so that there is no copyright claim.

📌 Writing Rules ✍️
  • Overwriting, cutting, joint letters, or spelling mistakes are not allowed.
  • Writing should be clear and readable.
  • Write in capital letters only (cursive, Italian, and small letters not allowed).
  • Use of whitener or staples is not allowed.
  • Only one handwriting style is allowed.
